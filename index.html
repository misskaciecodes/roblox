Roblox

What Is It?

Roblox is a online multiplayer game. Created in 2006, it's original name was Dynablox! This loveable game was meant for young players, but not TOO young.

History

Roblox was created in 2006, as stated before, the original name was Dynablox. Owner David Baszucki and the Roblox Corporation, all came together and made a online kid-friendly game, made for many young players. Later on, the team decided to change the name to Roblox instead.
